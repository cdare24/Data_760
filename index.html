Where should new fuel stations be built?

From viewing where there are currently wind and solar projects in Kansas we can see that there are not many solar projects in the state. Wind is a much more dominant renewable energy, which makes some sense as Kansas is a part of the 'Tornado Alley'. My recommendation is that a new fuel station should be built in Ford county. Ford county has the largest number of wind projects ongoing making it a strong choice for connection with clean energy, it has a large population of close to 35,000 people and sees the intersection of 3 major highways. This means a fuel station would be able to benefit residents of Ford county and could be used for people who are passing through on multiple highways.
